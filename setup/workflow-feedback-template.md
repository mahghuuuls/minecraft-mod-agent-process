# Workflow Feedback

This document records friction, corrections, and improvement ideas discovered while using the workflow on a specific mod.

It is a project-specific runtime artifact. Keep it under:

```text
workspace/documentation/workflow-feedback.md
```

Do not treat entries as approved process changes. They are input for a later, separate workflow-improvement session outside the active mod-development workflow.

## Open Feedback

### FB-001: Short Title

**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD  
**Workflow:** Initial Development | Existing Mod Assessment | Change Cycle  
**Stage:** Stage name or checkpoint  
**Status:** Open | Applied | Rejected | Superseded  
**Severity:** Low | Medium | High  
**Category:** Clarity | Missing Step | Wrong Default | Too Heavy | Too Light | Agent Behavior | Artifact | Tooling | Other

**Trigger:**  
What happened in the interaction that revealed this issue or improvement opportunity.

**Observed Problem:**  
What was confusing, inefficient, wrong, redundant, too strict, or missing.

**Suggested Change:**  
What should change in the workflow, stage, guideline, setup file, or agent behavior.

**Affected Files or Areas:**

- Unknown

**Evidence or Example:**  
Optional concise example, quote, command, artifact name, or behavior that explains the issue.

**Owner Notes:**  
Optional owner preference or decision.

## Applied Feedback

Move entries here only after they are reflected in the process repository by a separate workflow-improvement update or intentionally resolved.

## Rejected Or Deferred Feedback

Move entries here when the owner decides not to apply them now. Record the reason.
